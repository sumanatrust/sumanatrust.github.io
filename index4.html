SUMANA TRUST

Sumana Trust is a non-profit organization. Our vision is to achieve a balance in the society by meeting the social, medical and educational needs of the under-privileged people.

About Us

Sumana foundation is a non-profit, non-government organisation founded by the doctors of the 1992 batch of Kilpauk medical college.

Our Mission

  • To provide monetary aid and/or good quality medical services to people who belong to economically under-privileged sections of the society.
  • To provide monetary help and/or good background for basic and advanced education for young students and scholars who can not afford a standard education due to inadequate financial support.
  • To assist in promoting financially independent jobs for destitute women by training and empowering them in job-specific skills.

Goals

  • To select villages, rural areas or other areas, where the ideas of the foundation can be implemented.
  • To identify the people in the selected areas for whom the foundation will provide its aid.
  • To define a time-frame for the immediate implementation and continuous maintenance of the programs of the foundation.

Objectives

  • By getting statistics from various sources about places, where one or more of the members of the foundation can easily access and do a ground work of the situation and its needs.
  • By visiting public health centers and government schools in the selected areas to gather information regarding the needs of the people.
  • By conducting or organising medical camps in those areas to identify the health problems for which medical assistance will be needed.
  • By conducting educational fairs in schools of those areas, where modern educational tools can be introduced and career counseling can be done
